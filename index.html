2. Match competitors with a financing option and/or promotional offer

  • Competitor research showed several local competitors actively promote financing (monthly payment plans) and seasonal/promotional offers to homeowners.
  • Buckshot currently has no comparable offer on the site or in ads — a gap right at the decision stage.
  • Recommend: add a financing option (e.g. "low monthly payments" or "0% for X months" via a financing partner) and/or a limited-time promo (e.g. seasonal discount or free upgrade), shown on the landing page and in ad copy/sitelinks.
  • Why it matters: on a $10k–30k job, financing removes the biggest objection — and competitors are already using it to win price-sensitive leads.
